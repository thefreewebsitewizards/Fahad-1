Our Services

For five years and beyond, we have become trusted partners

with our clients in understanding the five basic functions of construction management and delivering comprehensive building solutions.

Construction Management Functions

Scope Definition

Defining and establishing the scope of the construction project with clear objectives and deliverables.

Pre-Construction Planning

Comprehensive project planning including preparation, scheduling, phasing plans, budgeting, cost-modeling, and subcontractor qualification.

On-Site Execution

Project execution including scheduling, quality control, safety management, subcontractor management, status reporting and change management.

Monitoring & Control

Continuous monitoring and controlling of the project to ensure quality, timeline, and budget compliance.

Project Closing

Closing of the project related to post-construction activities, final inspections, and client handover.

Benefits for Clients

Strong relationships lie at the heart of MCDON CIVIL ENGINEERING LTD, interactions with its clients. Mcdon builds and maintains those relationships by being versatile when it comes to budgeting, scheduling, and planning, while being responsive and communicative throughout the life cycle of a project.

Mcdon participation in the project begins immediately upon project award, and continues through the three phases of the project: preconstruction, construction, and the postconstruction and warranty period. Extensive preconstruction services include budgeting, preparing and maintaining a master schedule, evaluating trade contractors, and developing a project execution plan that will drive the onsite activities during construction phase.

Communication and Responsiveness

Communication is key on every project. During construction, Mcdon will do everything from controlling costs to executing an effective safety plan, and will provide timely and accurate project status reports. Mcdon'S responsibility for excellent client service continues through the postconstruction period. The project team will be the leaders on all postconstruction items, especially any warranty items. This approach ensures that clients always know who to go to for assistance with any issue.

Strength and Knowledge

With a bonding capacity that has no established upper limit, Mcdon'S financial strength gives you peace of mind. PCL has an unmatched supply of experienced labor, the ability to self-perform its work where necessary.

Mcdon'S project team will always be responsive and will remain flexible and versatile throughout the life cycle of the build.
